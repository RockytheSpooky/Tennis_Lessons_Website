About the Program

ThriveUp Tennis is a nonprofit that offers free tennis lessons to underserved communities. ThriveUp's mission is to empower students by building confidence, promoting physical health, and learning teamwork. The tennis program is beginner-friendly and led by ThriveUp's founder and president, Sreemat Kandasamy, who has over 7 years of experience in the Sport of Tennis.
