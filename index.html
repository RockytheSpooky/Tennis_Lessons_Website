ThriveUp Tennis

Free tennis lessons for underprivileged youth

About the Program

ThriveUp Tennis is a nonprofit that offers free tennis lessons to underserved families ThriveUp's mission is to empower students by building confidence, promoting physical health, and learning teamwork. The tennis program is beginner-friendly and led by ThriveUp's founder and president, Sreemat Kandasamy, who has over 7 years of experience in the Sport of Tennis.

What to bring

To ensure a successful and smooth experience, students are kindly requested to bring their own water bottles, tennis rackets, and tennis shoes. Please stay active with emails, as additional information will be sent through email
