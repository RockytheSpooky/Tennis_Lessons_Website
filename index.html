About the Program

ThriveUp Tennis is a nonprofit that offers free tennis lessons to underserved communities. ThriveUp's mission is to empower students by building confidence, promoting physical health, and learning teamwork. The program is beginner-friendly and led by Sreemat Kandasamy.
